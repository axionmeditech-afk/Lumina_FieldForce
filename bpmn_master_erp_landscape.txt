LANE: Market / Customer Demand
Marketing: Run Campaigns / Referrals / Field Outreach
System: Capture Inquiry / Lead
Sales: Qualify Lead and Capture Requirement
Sales: Create Quotation / Proposal
Customer: Review Proposal and Confirm Order Intent

LANE: Sales and Order Management
Sales: Convert Prospect to Customer
Sales: Create Sales Order
Accounts: Check Credit / Payment Terms if Required
Planning: Check Stock and Delivery Commitment
Gateway (Exclusive): Make to Stock Available?
If Yes -> Reserve Finished Goods for Dispatch
If No -> Trigger Procurement / Manufacturing Planning

LANE: Procurement and Production
Procurement: Raise Raw Material Requirement
Procurement: Create Purchase Order to Vendor
Vendor: Supply Raw Material
Store: Receive Material and Create GRN
QC: Perform Incoming Material Inspection
Manufacturing: Execute Production Plan
QC: Perform In-Process and Final Quality Check

LANE: Dispatch and Commercial Closure
Warehouse: Pack and Prepare Shipment
Logistics: Dispatch Goods / Service Delivery
Customer: Accept Delivery
Accounts: Create Sales Invoice
Accounts: Receive and Reconcile Payment
Support: Handle Complaint / Service if Needed
Management: Review Order Closure and Margin

LANE: Supporting Functions
Admin: Configure Masters, Roles, Branches and Controls
HR: Onboard Employees and Maintain Workforce Records
Accounts: Post Ledger, Tax, Payable and Receivable Entries
Management: Review Dashboards, Audit Logs and KPIs
